Courses by Department

This is a sampling of School of Art courses; not all of which are offered each semester or year.
Courses numbered 100-499 are undergraduate courses offered through Yale College, with some sections
reserved for Preliminary Graphic Design students. Courses numbered 500-999 are graduate level courses for
MFA students. In cases of elective courses that are not core requirements, permission of
the instructor is required to enroll.
